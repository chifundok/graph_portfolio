About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an intensive 8-week online programme that teaches no-code AI tools for automating routine tasks, analysing data and creating content.

What You'll Learn

  • Automate document, spreadsheet & data tasks
  • Organise knowledge & accelerate literature reviews
  • Build custom chatbots to support research projects
  • Create professional visuals & presentations
  • Design & present a hands-on capstone project

Course Snapshot

  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Next Cohort: 13 July 2025
  • Format: Live online workshops & help sessions
  • Workshop Options: Fridays 15:00 GMT or Sundays 17:00 GMT
